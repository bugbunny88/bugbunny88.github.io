About Me

Family Life & Career Journey

Family Life

Born in Lynwood, California, and raised by Honduran immigrant parents, I'm a first-generation Latina with a strong connection to my roots. Outside of work, you’ll find me spending quality time with my family, cooking and baking sourdough bread, and focusing on personal growth through meditation and journaling.

Career Journey

I began my career as a Master Esthetician, graduating from Aveda Institute in 2017 and running my own waxing business for six years. After experiencing burnout, I felt the need for a new direction and decided to explore a career in tech, inspired by my husband, who is also in the tech industry. In early 2023, I completed a Software Testing and Quality Bootcamp and landed my first role as a Quality Specialist. Since then, I've progressed to Quality Engineer II, gaining hands-on experience and advancing my skills along the way. My journey as a woman in tech without a formal degree has come with its challenges, but through persistence, ongoing learning, and valuable mentorship, I’m now on track to achieve my ultimate goal of becoming an Automation Engineer.
