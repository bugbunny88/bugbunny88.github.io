Tech Moms

Program Reflection

1. What about your website are you most proud of?

I'm most proud of the dark mode toggle. It took me the longest to figure out and build, but with help from YouTube and ChatGPT, I made it happen. It’s still a work in progress, but seeing it come to life was so rewarding!

2. What are your plans after the program? Are you leaning toward a specific role? What will your steps be to get there?

After the program, I’ll continue to improve my coding skills and work toward a role as an Automation Engineer. I’ve created a roadmap to achieve this using the SMART framework, which you can view in the references.

3. What are your key takeaways? What did you learn that will have the biggest impact on you?

I gained a solid understanding of frontend technologies like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, which will help me write more precise test cases, anticipate common frontend issues, and assist developers in fixing minor bugs more efficiently.

4. What were the biggest challenges you faced during the program? How did you get through them?

Turning designs into code was challenging. I experimented with different CSS properties and even restructured my code a few times to get the output I wanted.

Return with fresh eyes. Taking a break can make a big difference. Sometimes, stepping away and coming back the next day brought new ideas and clarity—at times, I even dreamed of solving the dark mode toggle!
